Delete your Tilawa account

You can delete your Tilawa account and associated profile data using either method below.

In the app (recommended)

  1. Open Tilawa and sign in with Google.
  2. Go to Settings → Your account → Delete account.
  3. Confirm deletion. This removes your Firebase account and Firestore profile.

Request by email

If you cannot access the app, email the address listed on the Google Play store listing with the subject Delete my Tilawa account and include the Google email address tied to your Tilawa sign-in.

We process deletion requests without requiring you to stay signed in. Verified support purchase records may be retained in anonymized form for fraud prevention.
