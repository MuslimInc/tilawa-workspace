Tilawa Privacy Policy

Tilawa is a Quran listening and worship companion. This page summarizes how we handle personal data in the mobile app.

Data we collect

  • Google account profile used for sign-in (name, email, photo URL)
  • Device notification token for optional reminders
  • Approximate or precise location when you use prayer times or qibla
  • App activity analytics and crash diagnostics to improve stability
  • Google Play purchase tokens for optional Support Tilawa contributions

How we use data

Data is used to authenticate you, sync your profile, deliver features you request, verify support purchases, and improve app reliability. Tilawa does not show ads and does not sell personal data.

Retention and deletion

You can delete your account from Tilawa Settings or request deletion on the web: Account deletion.

Verified support purchase records may be retained in anonymized form for fraud prevention as allowed by Google Play policies.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact the Tilawa team through the support channel listed on the Google Play store listing.
